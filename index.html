About WeatherWise AI

At WeatherWise AI, I explore the intersection of machine learning, physics, and atmospheric science to advance weather forecasting, especially for data-scarce regions like Uganda. I am passionate about creating models that not only predict but understand the weather by embedding physical principles into AI systems.

With Uganda's growing climate challenges — floods, droughts, and shifting weather patterns — accurate, timely weather forecasting is critical. Through research, innovation, and data-driven solutions, I aim to contribute toward climate resilience, agriculture security, and disaster preparedness.

About Me

I’m Immaculate Kamusiime, a Data Scientist, Engineer, and PhD Researcher at Makerere University, Uganda. I specialize in machine learning, remote sensing, data analytics, physics-informed models, and AI for environmental sciences.

Through academic research, industrial projects, and innovation initiatives, I have built expertise in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights for real-world problems.
